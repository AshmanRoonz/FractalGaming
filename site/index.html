What is Fractal Gaming?

Games you can read like a text.

Most games are black boxes. Ours aren't. Open the file, hit View Source, and the entire thing is in front of you. The point is the craft.

⊙

Single-file games

One HTML, one open. No build pipeline, no installer, no launcher. The whole game ships as a readable artifact.

◉

P2P multiplayer

Mesh networking through WebRTC. No central server, no account system, no telemetry hose pointed at your machine.

●

No dark patterns

No lootboxes, no FOMO timers, no engineered confusion at checkout. The boundary is: is this good for the player.

○

Open source code

MIT license on the code. Fork it, learn from it, ship your own version. Attribution is appreciated, not required.

Φ

Framework-driven

Every system in the engine maps onto a layer of the Circumpunct Framework. The architecture is the philosophy.

—

Faithful across time

If a game works offline today, it works offline next year. Updates are maintenance, not engagement bait.

Ethics policy, on the record

Five pillars, applied to gamedev.

These constrain what we ship. They are the actual filter, not slogans.

○

Good

What ships has to be good for the player. The boundary asks: is this good for the person on the other side of the screen.

Φ

Right

What sits between us and the player (purchase, account, data) mediates fairly. The price is the price.

—

Faithful

Promises hold across time. Offline stays offline. Account state belongs to the player.

•

True

Marketing matches the game. AI involvement disclosed. When something breaks publicly, we say what changed.

⊙

Agreement

The four above, met by both sides. We earn the player's agreement; we don't engineer around its absence.
